What is sound?
--------------

-   Sound = pressure waves
-   Vary in frequency, amplitude
-   Requires a physical medium
-   Works in the dark, over long distances, out of sight lines

Function of ossicles, stapedius
-------------------------------

-   Ossicles amplify
    -   Air thinner than cochlear fluid
-   Muscles dampen
